Welcome to the ProjectAchilles blog.

ProjectAchilles is a purple team platform for continuous security validation: we deploy
agents to real endpoints, execute real attack techniques mapped to MITRE ATT&CK, and
measure whether your defenses actually detected them.

This blog is where we will share what we learn building it — detection engineering
patterns, purple team methodology, agent internals, and honest write-ups of what worked
and what did not.

## What to expect

- **Engineering deep dives** — how the platform works under the hood
- **Detection engineering** — turning test results into better coverage
- **Purple team practice** — methodology for continuous validation programs
